"Your gift of blood saves lives."

Thank you for donating blood!

Here are some important precautions and care tips to follow after blood donation:

  • Rest for at least 10-15 minutes after donation.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water and avoiding alcohol.
  • Avoid strenuous activities for the rest of the day.
  • Do not smoke for at least 30 minutes after donating.
  • Enjoy a light snack and a non-alcoholic beverage provided by the blood bank.
  • If you experience any discomfort, inform the medical staff immediately.
